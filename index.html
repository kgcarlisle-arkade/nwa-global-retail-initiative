The Challenge

The opportunity is here.
The connection isn't.

"Without intentional infrastructure to connect entrepreneurs, talent, and industry, the region risks underutilizing its full innovation potential."
Today, without Land + Launch NWA:
  • Companies from outside the region conduct cold outreach or visit Bentonville for 1–2 days of meetings — then leave
  • Introductions are made informally, without structure or sustained follow-through
  • No formalized pathway connects founders to workspace, pilot opportunities, regulatory navigation, or workforce
  • High-growth companies default to Austin, Atlanta, New York, and Toronto — ecosystems that actively recruit and integrate them
The Solution

A structured platform.
A connected ecosystem.
A place to grow.

Land + Launch NWA is a cohort-based and community-integrated initiative supporting 6–12 growth-stage B2B technology companies serving Northwest Arkansas' anchor industries — consumer commerce, food and agriculture, and logistics and transportation — through a structured six-month engagement. Led by the Greater Bentonville Area Chamber of Commerce. Delivered by Arkade.

Participating companies receive dedicated support, curated introductions to enterprise partners, investors, and ecosystem leaders, physical workspace at Arkade, and full integration into the regional innovation ecosystem.

Land + Launch NWA is built around the founders themselves — providing the workspace, programming, mentorship, and ecosystem access that growth-stage companies need to commit to Northwest Arkansas long-term.

The Partnership

Two organizations. Each essential. One shared mission.

Fiduciary & Civic Lead
Greater Bentonville Area Chamber of Commerce
Brings the civic mandate and institutional credibility to make this work at a regional scale. Its Bold Bentonville strategic plan explicitly names this kind of coordinated ecosystem infrastructure as a priority — with a proven 501(c)(3) fiduciary structure for public-private initiatives. The Chamber does not just endorse this initiative. It has been calling for it.
Program Delivery Partner
Arkade
Brings the platform and commercial expertise to deliver what the ecosystem has been missing. Designed to help businesses from outside the region gain access to NWA's innovation ecosystem, Arkade connects founders to the mentors, advisors, industry leaders, and partners they need to succeed. Arkade does not replicate what exists — it delivers what no other entity in the region is currently structured to do.
On governance and accountability: All grant funds will be administered through the Chamber, allocated strictly toward approved programmatic activities, and reported against clear KPIs on a quarterly basis. Annual public impact reporting will be produced. The initiative is designed as catalytic funding with a built-in sustainability path — not a permanent operating subsidy.
