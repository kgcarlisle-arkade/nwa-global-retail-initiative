Budget Assumptions
  • 3% annual inflation applied across all line items
  • Year 1 is 100% WFF-funded to build infrastructure and engage the first cohort
  • Year 2 tapers to 65% WFF / 35% Arkade + Chamber match
  • Year 3 tapers to 30% WFF / 70% earned revenue, sponsorship, and match
  • Arkade absorbs $60,000/yr in utilities, legal, accounting, and operating software outside the grant budget
  • All grant funds administered through the Chamber as fiduciary; quarterly reporting against KPIs
  • Monthly equal distribution within each budget category
